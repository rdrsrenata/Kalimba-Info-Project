which model?

Kalimbas are basically divided according to their base material, that can be made of wood or acrylic. Another division is related to whether this base has a hole or not (the hole makes the sound louder). In addition, kalimbas are also divided according to their number of keys: 8, 17 or 21 keys. For beginners, the best kalimbas are those made of wood, with a hole and with 17 keys.


where to find tabs?

Kalimba is not a famous instrument and because of that it can be a little difficult to find tabs. You can try to search for tabs on google, directly on the website KalimbaTabs or even create your own tabs and make it available to the comunity.
